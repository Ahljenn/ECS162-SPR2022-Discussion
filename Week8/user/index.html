The Free Peoples of Middle-Earth

The Free Peoples of the World were those people who were not under Sauron's domination. By the later Third Age Sauron had Orcs, Trolls and other creatures under his control, and other peoples like the Men of Darkness enslaved or allied with him. As such, the Free Peoples were: Dwarves, Elves, Ents, Hobbits, and Men.

Ents

Hobbits
